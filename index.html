About The Foundation

The HOLE Foundation (Honest Oversight and Legal Ethics) is a Delaware 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to government transparency, accountability, and ethical governance.

Our Mission

We are committed to advancing transparency and accountability through strategic advocacy, public education, and institutional reform. Our work focuses on ensuring that government institutions serve the public interest with integrity and openness.

Founded to address systematic challenges in government accountability, we pursue our mission through research, advocacy, and strategic legal action when necessary.
